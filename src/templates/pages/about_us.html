About us

Jarvis was founded by four passionate computer science students at the University of Reykjavík. They found a common challenge faced by job seekers worldwide, the task of going through countless platforms to see if they are hiring people. Determined to fix this problem, they embarked on a journey to create a solution that would make job seeking easier.

Welcome to Jarvis - Where your next career adventure begins.
